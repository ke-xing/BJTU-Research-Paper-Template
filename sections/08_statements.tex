\section*{\normalfont\color{bjtublue}Broader Impact \& Reproducibility}
\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Broader Impact \& Reproducibility}

\begin{broaderimpact}
Open-sourcing capable LLMs democratizes reasoning research but may also lower
the barrier to misuse (e.g., automated disinformation). We mitigate this by
(a)~releasing safety-tuned checkpoints only, (b)~publishing a detailed
model card, and (c)~providing an evaluation of refusal behavior on
adversarial prompts.
\end{broaderimpact}

\begin{reproducibility}
All training data recipes, hyper-parameters, and evaluation scripts are
released at \url{https://github.com/bjtu-ai/BJTU-LLM}. Model weights are
available under an OpenRAIL-M license. See Appendix~\ref{app:hparams} for
the complete list of hyper-parameters.
\end{reproducibility}
