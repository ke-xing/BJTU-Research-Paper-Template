\section{Related Work}
\label{sec:related}

\paragraph{Scaling laws for language models}
Kaplan et al.~\citep{kaplan2020scaling} and Hoffmann et
al.~\citep{hoffmann2022chinchilla} established empirical laws describing how
model quality scales with parameters, data, and compute.

\paragraph{Reasoning in LLMs}
Chain-of-thought prompting~\citep{wei2022cot} unlocked latent reasoning in
sufficiently large models. Subsequent work explored self-consistency, tool
use, and search-augmented reasoning.

\paragraph{Efficient attention}
Sparse and linear attention variants have been proposed to reduce the
quadratic cost of the standard Transformer~\citep{vaswani2017attention},
including FlashAttention~\citep{dao2022flashattention}.

\paragraph{RLHF and process rewards}
Reinforcement learning from human feedback has become the dominant paradigm
for aligning LLMs. Recent work argues that dense, process-level rewards
outperform sparse outcome rewards on multi-step tasks.
